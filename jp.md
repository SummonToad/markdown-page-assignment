## Plot Summary

A wealthy entrepreneur, **John Hammond**, invites paleontologists and experts to preview his new theme park, where dinosaurs have been resurrected through cloning.  
When the park’s security systems fail, the island becomes a dangerous and unpredictable fight for survival.

> “Life finds a way.”

- **Dr. Alan Grant** — Paleontologist  
- **Dr. Ellie Sattler** — Paleobotanist

## Key Themes

### Scientific Ethics  
The film raises questions about the morality of cloning and genetic engineering.

### Chaos Theory  
Ian Malcolm’s explanations highlight the unpredictability of complex systems.

### Humanity vs. Nature  
No matter how advanced technology becomes, nature remains powerful and uncontrollable.

## Behind the Scenes

- The film used a groundbreaking combination of CGI and practical animatronics.  
- The T-Rex animatronic frequently malfunctioned due to the water in the rain sequences.  
- Based on the novel by **Michael Crichton**, who also co-wrote the screenplay.  
- Many dinosaur sounds were created by mixing animal noises such as elephants, tigers, and alligators.
