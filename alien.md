## Plot Summary

The crew of the commercial starship **Nostromo** is awakened from hypersleep to investigate a mysterious transmission.  
When crew member *Kane* encounters an alien organism, things go horribly wrong, leading to one of the most terrifying survival stories in sci-fi history.

> “In space, no one can hear you scream.”

## Key Themes

### Survival Horror  
The film blends sci-fi and horror to create a tense, claustrophobic atmosphere.

### Corporate Exploitation  
The company (“The Company”) prioritizes the alien lifeform over human survival.

### Isolation  
The characters are alone in deep space with no hope of rescue.
