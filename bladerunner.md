## Plot Summary

Set thirty years after the original *Blade Runner*, the story follows **K**, a replicant blade runner who uncovers a long-buried secret that threatens to destabilize society.  
His discovery leads him to **Rick Deckard**, who has been missing for decades.

> “Sometimes, to love someone, you have to be a stranger.”

## Main Characters

- **K (Joe)** — Replicant blade runner and protagonist  
- **Rick Deckard** — Former blade runner from the original film  
- **Joi** — K’s holographic companion  
- **Niander Wallace** — CEO of Wallace Corporation  
- **Luv** — Wallace’s replicant enforcer

## Key Themes

### Identity  
The film examines what it means to be human, questioning memory, emotion, and purpose.

### Artificial Life  
Replicants struggle for autonomy and recognition of their humanity.

### Loneliness  
Many characters confront the emotional emptiness of their existence.
